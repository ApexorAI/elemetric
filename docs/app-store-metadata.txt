DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
-----------------------------
Elemetric is the compliance documentation app built for licensed Australian tradespeople — plumbers, gas fitters, electricians, HVAC technicians, and carpenters working under BPC Victoria and Australian Standards.

Stop relying on paper checklists. Photograph your work on site, let our AI analyse it against AS/NZS 3500, AS/NZS 5601, and AS/NZS 3000 series standards, and generate a tamper-evident PDF compliance report in under 60 seconds.

WHAT ELEMETRIC DOES:
• Photo-first compliance workflow — photograph every stage of your work
• AI review against AS/NZS 3500 series (plumbing), AS/NZS 5601.1:2022 (gas), AS/NZS 3000:2018 (electrical), AS/NZS 1684:2010 (carpentry)
• Structured rule engine for AS/NZS 3500.4:2025 (heated water services) — depth of cover, bedding thickness, trench width, contamination zones, corrosion protection, freezing conditions, roof space clearance
• Tamper-evident PDF compliance certificate with QR code verification
• Share reports directly with clients, building surveyors, or the BPC via PDF, WhatsApp, or SMS
• 7-year liability record — all documentation stored securely to Supabase cloud
• Invoice generator — send professional invoices pre-filled with job details
• Near miss reporting — log site hazards for WHS compliance
• Property passport — searchable compliance history per address
• Compliance chatbot — ask questions about BPC requirements, AS/NZS clauses, and licence obligations
• Employer portal — manage subcontractors, verify licences, ABN, and insurance expiry
• Client portal — share reports with property owners without requiring an account
• Timesheet — log hours, calculate weekly pay, link to jobs
• Training mode — practice compliance photo reviews with AI coaching
• Full accessibility support — VoiceOver and dynamic type

WHO IT'S FOR:
Elemetric is built for licensed tradespeople in Victoria and nationally — particularly those working under BPC (Building and Plumbing Commission of Victoria) oversight. It's equally useful for plumbers, gas fitters, electricians, HVAC technicians, and carpenters who need to produce and store compliant job documentation.

STANDARDS COVERED:
• AS/NZS 3500.1:2025 — Plumbing and drainage: Water services
• AS/NZS 3500.2:2025 — Plumbing and drainage: Sanitary plumbing and drainage
• AS/NZS 3500.4:2025 — Plumbing and drainage: Heated water services
• AS/NZS 5601.1:2022 — Gas installations
• AS/NZS 3000:2018 — Wiring rules
• AS 1684.2:2010 — Residential timber-framed construction
• AS/NZS 5149.1:2016 — Refrigerating systems

COMPLIANCE DISCLAIMER:
Elemetric is a documentation aid. Final compliance responsibility rests solely with the licensed tradesperson. Reports reference AS/NZS standards as enforced under Australian building legislation.
